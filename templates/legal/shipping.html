Shipping Policy

Last updated: 16 March 2026

Swann currently delivers orders within the United Arab Emirates.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation.

Delivery Time

  • Estimated delivery time is usually between 4–7 business days depending on the delivery location and courier service.
  • Business days exclude weekends and public holidays.

Incorrect Address

  • Customers are responsible for providing the correct shipping address. Orders returned due to incorrect or incomplete addresses may require additional shipping charges for re-delivery.

Shipping Charges

  • Shipping charges will be calculated and displayed during checkout.

Delays

  • Delivery times may vary due to public holidays, courier delays, or circumstances beyond our control.

Contact
