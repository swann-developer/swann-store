Refund and Return Policy

Last updated: 16 March 2026

At Swann, customer satisfaction is important to us.

Returns

Items may be returned within 7 days of delivery if they meet the following conditions:

  • The item is unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging and tags are intact
  • The item was not purchased as a final sale item

Non-Returnable Items

Returns may not be accepted for:

  • Used or damaged items caused by the customer
  • Items without original packaging or tags

Refunds

  • Once the returned item is received and inspected, we will notify you regarding the approval of the refund.
  • If approved, refunds will be issued to the original payment method within a reasonable processing time.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to an incorrect or defective item.
